Property Subscription Refund Policy

G-WEALTH KING REPUBLIC LTD (R.C NO: 9087307)

1 POLICY PURPOSE

This policy sets out the terms, conditions, and procedures governing refund requests that may arise from the discontinuation of property subscription payments by subscribers (investors) of G-WEALTH KING REPUBLIC LTD.

This policy is binding on all subscribers and forms an integral part of every Property / Land Subscription Agreement.

2 ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUND

A subscriber may apply for a refund only under the following conditions:

  • Voluntary discontinuation of subscription payments
  • Inability to continue payment due to personal or financial reasons
  • Relocation or change in circumstances
  • Any other reason deemed acceptable by Management

Note: Refunds are not automatic and remain subject to approval under this policy.

3 NON-REFUNDABLE DEDUCTIONS

The subscriber expressly acknowledges that the following fees are strictly non-refundable:

  • Registration fees
  • Administrative fees
  • Documentation fees
  • Processing charges
  • Any other incidental or operational costs already incurred

Only refundable portions of payments (if any) shall be considered.

4 REFUND APPLICATION PROCEDURE

The subscriber must complete the Property Subscription Refund Request Form in full. All required information and bank details must be accurate and verifiable. Incomplete or false information will result in rejection or delay.

5 MODE OF REFUND

Refunds shall be made only to the subscriber's personal bank account. Cash refunds are strictly prohibited. Third-party accounts shall not be accepted.

6 PROCESSING PERIOD

Approved refunds shall be processed within 30 – 90 working days from the date of approval. The subscriber acknowledges that processing time does not attract interest or compensation.

7 TERMINATION OF PROPERTY RIGHTS

Upon approval and execution of a refund, all associated property rights, allocations, or reservations tied to the original subscription shall be automatically terminated and returned to G-WEALTH KING REPUBLIC LTD.
