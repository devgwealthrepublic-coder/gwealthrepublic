The GWealth Protocol

Our four-pillar framework ensures that every investment is shielded by the highest standards of Nigerian property law.

01

Corporate Acquisition

We only acquire land directly from original family heads or state allocations, conducting a 50-year title history search before initial payment.

02

Perimeter Mapping

Our registered surveyors establish beaconing and scope coordinates with the Surveyor General's office to eliminate encroachment risks.

03

Title Clearance

We formalize Gazettes, Certificates of Occupancy, or Deeds of Assignment, ensuring all government levies are settled in full.

04

Secure Transfer

Upon purchase, you receive a complete legal kit including a Registered Survey and a Deed of Assignment executed by our legal team.
