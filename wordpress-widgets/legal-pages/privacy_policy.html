Privacy Policy & Data Security Covenant

Effective July 2026. Mapped directly to physical real estate operations.

Data Collection Scope

At G-Wealth Republic (operating officially as GWealth Properties), we collect only standard information strictly necessary to complete layout land transactions, assign concrete survey beacons, coordinate site inspections, and process offline transactions.

Logged Parameters:

  • Full legal names corresponding to government IDs.
  • Active WhatsApp coordinates for logistical communications.
  • Billing email addresses for official receipts and contracts.
  • Direct bank wire receipt files (PNG/JPG/PDF) strictly for verifying manual escrow deposits.

How We Protect Your Data (Decoupled Sync Flow)

Your privacy is fortified by military-grade, decoupled data architectures. Personal customer documents and bank receipt coordinates completely bypass public WordPress archives. All physical receipts and customer details are pipelined directly to secure Express APIs and hosted on highly encrypted, isolated media networks.

No Sharing Policy: G-Wealth Republic has never, and will never, sell, lease, or share your transactional data to external lists, data brokers, or unauthorized third-party agencies.

Comments & Media

When visitors leave comments on our blog, we collect the data shown in the comments form, along with the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to assist in spam detection.

An anonymized string created from your email address (a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to verify usage. After comment approval, your profile picture is visible to the public. If you upload images to the website, avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS).

Cookies & Embedded Content

If you leave a comment on our site, you may opt-in to saving your name, email, and website in cookies for convenience. These last for one year.

For administrative portals, temporary cookies are set to determine browser compatibility. Logging in sets cookies to save login information and display choices (lasting two days to a year). Articles on this site may also include embedded content (e.g., videos or maps) which behaves exactly as if you visited the source website, and may utilize their own tracking cookies.

Data Rights & Retention

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely to recognize and approve follow-up comments automatically. If you have an account or have left comments, you can request an exported file of your personal data or request the erasure of any personal data we hold.

Note: This does not include any data we are legally obliged to keep for administrative, real estate transaction, or security purposes.

Critical Legal Separation

G-Wealth Republic does not manage, process, pool, or hold public financial contributions, "daily contributions", "cooperative food programs", or "micro-savings". By using this platform, you acknowledge we are strictly real estate developers. We collect zero investment assets. Any transactions performed are explicitly for the purchase of physical land or property allocations.
