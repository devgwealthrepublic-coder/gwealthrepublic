This document maps out the exact coordinates and boundaries of your specific plot. It is lodged at the Surveyor General's office to mathematically prove the land exists and belongs to you.

The ultimate proof of transfer. This legal agreement unequivocally states that G-Wealth Republic has permanently transferred all rights, ownership, and interest of the land to you, the buyer.
